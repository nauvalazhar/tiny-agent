Basic HTML Structure

The basic video element structure:

<video controls>
    <source src="video.mp4" type="video/mp4">
    <source src="video.webm" type="video/webm">
    Your browser does not support the video tag.
</video>

Common Attributes:

  • controls - Shows play/pause and other controls
  • autoplay - Starts playing automatically (usually requires muted)
  • muted - Starts muted (required for autoplay in most browsers)
  • loop - Repeats the video when it ends
  • poster - Image to show before video starts
  • width/height - Set dimensions
